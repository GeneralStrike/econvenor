eConvenor is for grassroots campaigns, non-government organisations (NGOs), progressive service providers and activist groups.

To use eConvenor your group must:

  •   have a progressive focus and
  •   be opposed to oppression.

You must not use eConvenor if your group:

  •   operates for profit
  •   is a government agency
  •   is a major political party or
  •   aims to spread religion.
