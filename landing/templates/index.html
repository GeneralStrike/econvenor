What does eConvenor do?

eConvenor is a free online tool for grassroots campaigns and community groups. It does the boring bits of group organising, so you can get on with the real work.  It's your virtual secretary, convenor and coordinator.

    • Keeps your group in the loop

    • Your account will hold contact details for your active members, so that you can send emails to the group, assign tasks, and set email reminders for them.

    • Pulls together your meeting agendas

    • You can use our agenda templates or create your own. The layout is done for you and you can estimate time for the meeting based on how long you'd like to spend on each item. Each agenda will automatically include any unfinished business or tasks from the previous meeting.

    • Creates and distributes minutes

    • When you're ready, crete a minutes template from the agenda with one click. You can print it and fill it out, or type the minutes straight in at the meeting. We've made sure there's clear space to record decisions and tasks, so they won't get lost, and you on't forget the point of the meeting.

    • Tracks and follows up tasks

    • A task list will be automatically created from your minutes, with the due date and the person who said they'd do it. You'll be able to see the whole list at any time, see any overdue tasks, and set automatic email reminders for those with tasks.

    • Collates and searches decisions

    • The decisions you make at your meetings ill be recorded in a master-list, so you can search through them later to find out what you decided last time, and look up the minutes from the time.

    • Organises your meeting records

    • *** Some text about keeping your agendas and minutes in one place and well organised rather than in a dropbox or googkle drive account.***

Who is eConvenor for?

eConvenor is for grassroots campaigns, non-government organisations (NGOs), progressive service providers and activist groups.  Your group must have a socially-just, progressive focus.

It's not for everyone though

We built eConvenor to make the world a better place.

You must not use eConvenor if your group:

  • is conservative or oppressive

  • operates for profit

  • is a government agency

  • is a major political party or

  • aims to spread religion.

eConvenor is free!

This is an open source project and we aim to make the eConvenor service available as widely as we can.   This means eConvenor is free for almost everyone, and we ask better funded groups to contribute financially.

From each according to their means

    • Most groups

    • Does your group have no budget or a tiny budget? eConvenor was built for you! You can use eConvenor completely free. No obligation, no worries.

    • Groups which can afford a donation

    • Groups who feel that they can afford to make a donation in return for using eConvenor should do so once each year.

    • Political parties and trade unions

    • Political parties and trade unions in high income countries who are progressive and committed to social justice can use eConvenor for a fee.

We rely on your honesty. We hope you will apply your ethics and contribute if you should. Donations help eConvenor grow and improve.
