What does eConvenor do?

eConvenor is a group-organising tool for grassroots campaigns and community groups. It handles the boring and time-consuming bits of organising a group, so you can get on with the real work.  It's your virtual secretary, convenor and coordinator.

    • Helps build meeting agendas

    • Create and edit agendas quickly and easily. You can add time limits for each item and eConvener will tell you what time the meeting should finish. Unfinished business and outstanding tasks are automatically included, then it's emailed out.

    • Creates easy-to-read minutes

    • One mouse click creates a minutes template from the agenda. Just fill it in. Once you finish the minutes they can be emailed out in seconds. The minutes show the decisions clearly, and there is a task table for quick reference.

    • Organises your meeting records

    • eConvenor keeps your group's agendas and minutes in one location. This ensures that your group's important documents are well organised, always available and are never lost or accidentally deleted.

    • Tracks and follows up tasks

    • Tasks are taken from meeting minutes, kept in a list and tracked so nothing is ever forgotten. When a task is nearly due, eConvenor emails a reminder to the person responsible. Tasks that are incomplete at the next meeting are automatically added to the agenda.

    • Collates and lists decisions

    • Decisions made by your group are copied from the minutes and collated into a master list. Shortly, decisions will be searchable by keyword or date. This is a useful tool to stop your group making the same decisions twice.

    • Holds your participants' details

    • eConvenor keeps a list of your group's participants, plus a summary page for every participant. Each participant's page shows their tasks.
