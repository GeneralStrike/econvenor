The eConvenor project

Goal

The project provides the eConvenor service to help activist groups around the world be better organised and more effective.

Background

The eConvenor project is a volunteer-run and not-for-profit.

The core of the project are Keira and Sky -- two activists from Melbourne, Australia.

The service went live in April 2014.
